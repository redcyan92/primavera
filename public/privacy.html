Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

OTRA is operated by Giuliano Rusciano, based in Barcelona, Spain. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

Contact: hello@otra.social

2. What data we collect and why

Data Why we collect it Legal basis
Email address To create and verify your account Contract
Festival notes The content you write to find a connection — description, artist, location, time Contract
Instagram / WhatsApp handle Only collected if you choose to share it with a confirmed mutual connection Consent
Age confirmation To verify you meet the 18+ requirement Legal obligation
App usage data (localStorage) To keep you logged in across sessions Legitimate interest

We do not collect photos, location data beyond what you write yourself, or payment information. We do not run ads.

3. Who we share your data with

  • Other users: Your festival notes are visible as described (Search notes only to compatible matches; Posts visible to all). Your Instagram or WhatsApp handle is shared only with a specific user with whom you have mutually confirmed a connection.
  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider. Data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • No one else. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers.

4. How long we keep your data

We keep your account and notes for as long as your account is active. If you ask us to delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days. Some data may be retained in backups for up to 90 days after deletion.

5. Your rights (EU and UK)

Under GDPR and UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent at any time for data processed on that basis (contact sharing)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@otra.social. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. In Spain: Agencia Española de Protección de Datos (AEPD). In the UK: the ICO.

6. Cookies and local storage

OTRA uses browser localStorage to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

7. Children

OTRA is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to how we handle your data, we will notify you by email before the changes take effect.

Contact: hello@otra.social
